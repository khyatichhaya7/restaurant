our best chef

Peter Gordon

Chef Gordon is credited as the “Grand Father” of different combinations in cooking. Peter pulled the limits of national cooking into another step forward.

Josh Emett and Giulio Sturla

New Zealand cook Josh Emett is valued among the best chef on the earth. Giulio Sturla is ranked as a standout amongst the most energizing gourmet experts in New Zealand

Annabel Langbein

One of New Zealand's most commended gourmet experts with numerous cookbooks and honors to her name

Yannick Alléno

Yannick Alléno is a French chef who is the current head chef of the restaurant Alléno Paris au Pavillon Ledoyen in Paris

Enrico Bartolini

The most starred chef in Italy, Enrico Bartolini is known for what he calls contemporary classic Italian cuisine. Bartolini also focuses on the nutritional element of his dishes and their visual appeal.

Pierre Gagnaire

Pierre Gagnaire is one of the best chefs in the world. His parents were restaurant owners and before taking over their business, he trained in some of the most prestigious kitchens worldwide such as Lucas Carton in Paris.
